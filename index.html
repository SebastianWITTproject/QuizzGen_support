‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI quiz generation work?

QuizzGen uses advanced AI technology to analyze your input text and automatically generate relevant questions. The AI understands context, identifies key concepts, and creates questions that test comprehension and knowledge retention.

What types of content work best for quiz generation?

QuizzGen works with any text content including articles, study materials, training documents, educational content, and more. The AI adapts to different content types and creates appropriate questions.

Can I edit the generated questions?

Yes! You can edit any generated question or answer, add new questions, or modify the quiz content to better suit your needs before saving or sharing.

How do I save my quizzes?

After generating a quiz, tap the "Save" button to add it to your personal collection. You can access all saved quizzes from the "My Quizzes" tab.

Can I share quizzes with others?

Absolutely! You can generate PDF versions of your quizzes and share them through messaging, email, or any sharing method available on your device.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all your quiz data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store your personal content or quiz data on our servers.

üîß Troubleshooting

App won't generate quizzes

Make sure you have a stable internet connection and that your input text is not empty. Try reducing the text length if you're experiencing issues.

Quizzes not saving

Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device. Try restarting the app if the issue persists.

PDF sharing not working

Check that you have granted the necessary permissions for file sharing. Try restarting the app and attempting to share again.

App crashes or freezes

Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem continues, restart your device and update to the latest version of the app.
